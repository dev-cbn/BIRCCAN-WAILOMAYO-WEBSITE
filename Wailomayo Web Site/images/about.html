Author: Enogela Bitrus Kaifas

Enogela Bitrus Kaifas is the visionary mind behind CBN Tech Coding School. A plaform built on passion, resilience, and a commmitment to impact. Born and raised in Makurdi, Benue State, Nigeria He has developed a a keen interest in inspiring youth to go into Tech at an early age.

With a background in Computer Science, Enogela Bitrus Kaifas began the journey with a simple idea to create something meaningful that would bridge gaps, solve problems and empower others. After facing some many challenges and setback, this experience became the fuel that drove the creation of CBN Tech Coding School.

Driven by a deep desire to provide value Enogela Bitrus Kaifas started the platform from scratch, often working late nights and learning through trial and error. What started as a one-person project quickly gained attention for it's authenticity, practical value, and user-first approach

Educational Background

Enogela Bitrus Kaifas has a solid academic foundation that laid the groundwork for the creation CBN Tech Coding School.

He began his Educational journey at Methodist Nursery & Primary School where the passion for learning and creativity began to grow. After completing basic education, He attended Saint Joseph Science & Technical College Makurdi where leadership skill and early interest in science where nurtured. Where he studied Electrical Installation & Maintenance Works where he was asign the Utility Prefect in his prestigious High School.

Driven by deep passion Enogela Bitrus Kaifas proceeded and earned a Bachelors Degree in Computer Science from Benue State University, Makurdi now known as Rev. Fr. Moses Orshio Adasu Universiy {MOAU}. During this time he was actively involved in Research Building his Coding Skills which helped shape a practicl understanding of the field
